Dive
into Tokyo

Shibuya, Shinjuku... If you want to visit those cities, you don't need to join us. Our Focus is alwasys on the connection with locals. We visit local-loved neighbourhood, while studying Japanese.

Our Classroom

Our classroom is the town itself.

Visit quiet, local spots full of history and nature. See the real Tokyo, far away from the busy tourist crowds.

Neighbourhood 01

🏢 Sangenjaya　三軒茶屋

Sangenjaya is a local favorite known for its friendly vibe and beautiful spots like Setagaya Park. It’s the perfect place to chat while walking through the greenery or enjoying a famous Imagawayaki sweet from a local shop.

Neighbourhood 02

⛩ Monzenn-Nakacho　門前仲町

Monzen-Nakacho is a historic district home to famous shrines and a warm, traditional atmosphere. Its calm streets and local charm make it the perfect, stress-free place to practice Japanese.

Neighbourhood 03

🍵 Kiyosumi-Shirakawa　清澄白河

Kiyosumi-Shirakawa is a peaceful area where old history meets modern coffee culture. You can walk through the beautiful Kiyosumi Garden and then enjoy a great cup of coffee at a local cafe.
