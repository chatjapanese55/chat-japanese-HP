What is Chat Japanese Tokyo?

Learn Japanese in a café.
Use it in the streets of Tokyo.

›

Chat Japanese Tokyo is a short-term Japanese school for travelers. You first build practical conversation skills with a friendly local teacher in a cozy café, then take those phrases straight into the city.

Practice real Japanese at places like Tsukiji Market, Asakusa street stalls, local shops, Meiji Shrine, and Sensō-ji Temple — while learning Japanese manners, history, and culture from someone who actually lives here.

Connect with Real Japan

What makes our approach
truly different

Conversation First

No textbooks, no grammar drills. Our lessons are built around real situations — ordering at a local restaurant, chatting with shopkeepers, navigating the city. You'll be speaking from day one.

Café Lessons × City Walk

Our classroom is a cozy local café. After the lesson, we hit the streets together — discovering Hidden Gems that tourists never find. We explore them side by side, just like locals do.

100% Local Instructors

Every single one of our instructors is a local Japanese person. Not just teachers — they're your guides to the real Japan. Their insider knowledge, stories, and connections are part of the curriculum.

Travel × Lessons

Learn as you move.
Move as you learn.

Why choose between sightseeing and studying? Each course is built around a city — so your classroom is the city itself.

Basic Info

Everything you need
to know

Everything you need to know before joining Chat Japanese Tokyo — from group sizes to cancellations.

Have a question that isn't here? Just reach out — we're happy to help you plan your trip. Email us at info@sym-arch.com.

01Programs

Chat Japanese Tokyo offers weekday courses, weekend courses, intensive programs, private language tours, and family plans. Each program is designed to help beginners use practical Japanese in real situations.

02Group Size

Regular group programs have a maximum of 8 participants. Private programs are designed for small private groups of up to 5 people. If your group has more than 5 people, please contact us in advance — mail to us (info@sym-arch.com). Additional fees may apply depending on the program.

03Food, Drinks & Transportation

Food, drinks, transportation, entrance fees, and personal purchases are not included unless clearly stated. Participants pay these costs separately.

04Meeting Point

The meeting point changes depending on the program, date, and route. We usually meet at a stylish local cafe or an easy-to-find nearby location. Discovering local cafes that visitors would not easily find on their own is part of the Chat Japanese Tokyo experience. We will send you the exact meeting point via WhatsApp after you complete your registration.

05Language Support

English support is available. Our programs are designed for international visitors and beginner Japanese learners.

06Booking Deadline

We recommend booking at least 1 week in advance. Last-minute requests may be accepted depending on availability — even bookings made the day before are often possible, so feel free to ask.

07Payment

Payment is made by credit card at the time of booking. Your reservation is confirmed after payment is completed.

08Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the program start time are eligible for a 100% refund.

09Rainy Weather

Outdoor activities may be adjusted in case of rain. Light rain programs can usually continue with indoor or covered routes. In severe weather, we may discuss schedule changes or alternative options.

10Customization

Some programs can be customized based on your interests, Japanese level, schedule, and group style. Private programs are the best option if you want a more personalized experience.

11Japanese Level

Our standard programs are mainly designed for beginners. If you are an intermediate learner or want more advanced practice, we recommend choosing a private program.

12Availability

Program availability depends on the date, instructor schedule, location, and group size. Please contact us before making fixed travel plans around a program.

13Course Start Day

The 1-Week Course and the Intensive Course start on Monday. You are welcome to join from a different day, but please note that there are no make-up lessons for any sessions missed before your start day.
