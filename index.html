What makes our approach
truly different

Conversation First

No textbooks, no grammar drills. Our lessons are built around real situations — ordering at a local restaurant, chatting with shopkeepers, navigating the city. You'll be speaking from day one.

Café Lessons × City Walk

Our classroom is a cozy local café. After the lesson, we hit the streets together — discovering Hidden Gems that tourists never find. We explore them side by side, just like locals do.

100% Local Instructors

Every single one of our instructors is a local Japanese person. Not just teachers — they're your guides to the real Japan. Their insider knowledge, stories, and connections are part of the curriculum.

A Week in Chat Japanese

What does a typical
day look like?

Every day is a mix of real conversation practice and city exploration — alongside fellow travelers from around the world.

Mon – Thu

Weekdays

Four mornings of immersive Japanese conversation in a cozy local café — then explore the city together.

9:00

Café Japanese Class

Conversation-first Japanese in a cozy local café. Small groups, friendly instructors, zero textbook pressure.

12:00

City Walking Tour

Explore the city with your instructor. Practice what you just learned — in real conversations, with real locals.

13:00

Free Time

The rest of the day is yours — explore, hang out with classmates from around the world, or just recharge.

Sat & Sun

Weekend

Two mornings of conversation practice followed by exclusive cultural experiences — only for Chat Japanese students.

9:00

Conversation Practice

Two hours of focused Japanese conversation with your group. Review the week's highlights and build confidence.

11:00

Cultural Activity

Dive into authentic Japanese culture — sushi-making, shodo calligraphy, matcha ceremony, and more.

13:00

Free Time

Head out with new friends from the US, Europe, Australia and beyond — your Japan adventure continues.
