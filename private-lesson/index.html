What is this?

A private Japanese learning experience — not just sightseeing

This is not just a sightseeing tour. It is a private Japanese learning experience where you use real phrases in real situations.

Instead of sitting in a classroom, you learn Japanese out in the city — ordering at a café, asking a shopkeeper a question, greeting people, and chatting about food and culture as you go. Your guide adapts everything to your level and your interests, so it feels natural from the very first minute.

  • Learn useful Japanese while walking around the city
  • Practice ordering, asking questions, greeting people, and talking about food or culture
  • Get support from a friendly guide
  • Adjusted to your level and interests
  • Good for complete beginners
