Course Overview

Four days. The Japanese you actually need.

The 1-Week Course is built for travelers who are short on time but still want to connect with Japan on their own terms. Over four weekday mornings, you'll build the conversational foundation that makes a real difference — ordering at a restaurant, introducing yourself, navigating a convenience store, reading the room.

Lessons are small-group and conversation-first, held in a relaxed Tokyo café environment. No textbooks, no memorization drills — just practical Japanese shaped around your trip.

✓
Mon – Thu, 3 hours/day — mornings only, afternoons free to explore
✓
Absolute beginner friendly — no prior Japanese required
✓
Cultural exchange included — meet local Japanese speakers in every session
✨

Many students upgrade to the Intensive Course — for just +$100, you also get Saturday & Sunday cultural activities (sushi making, shodo, matcha ceremony) included.
