Seasonal Highlights

🌸
Spring
Mar–May
Cherry blossoms (sakura). Mild 10–20°C. Crowds at parks.
💡 Book 3+ months ahead.
☀️
Summer
Jun–Aug
Hot & humid 28–35°C. Rainy season Jun–Jul. Festivals.
💡 Stay hydrated, carry fan.
🍁
Autumn
Sep–Nov
Comfortable 15–25°C. Fall foliage. Typhoon risk Sep.
💡 Best overall season.
❄️
Winter
Dec–Feb
Cold 0–10°C. Snow in Hokkaido. Fewer crowds.
💡 Great for skiing & onsen.
